Submission Guidelines

We seek stories of precision, consequence, and indelible truth.

Our Focus

Literary & Commercial Fiction

Clear Ledger is particularly interested in **Legal Thrillers**, **Financial Noir**, and **Character-Driven Mystery**. We look for manuscripts where the "ledger" is unbalanced—stories involving moral debt, hidden histories, and technical precision.

We are also open to **Professional Non-Fiction** that explores the intersection of ethics, history, and commerce.

The Query Bundle
  • 01.
    A Polished Query Letter: Include your genre, word count, and a brief "elevator pitch" of the work.
  • 02.
    10-Page Excerpt: The first ten pages of your manuscript, pasted into the body of the email. No attachments for initial queries.
  • 03.
    Synopsis: A 1-2 page summary of the complete narrative arc, including the ending.
Formatting

When our windows are open, we require standard manuscript formatting:

  • Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Double-spaced
  • 1-inch margins
  • Page numbers in the header

Wait for the Ledger to Open

We do not review unsolicited manuscripts during closed periods. Please follow our social media or join our newsletter to be notified of our next open reading window in late 2026.
