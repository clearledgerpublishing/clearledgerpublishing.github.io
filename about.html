Our Story

A commitment to transparency in the world of print.

The "Clear Ledger" Mission

Clear Ledger Publishing was founded on a simple, radical premise: that authors deserve a **Clear Ledger.** In an industry where royalty statements are often cryptic and payment cycles are opaque, we chose a different path.

Our founding mission was to provide 100% transparency regarding actual sales, actual expenses, and actual payments for every book we publish. We believe that when authors understand the business of their books, the entire industry thrives.

Our Core Pillars

  • 01 Transparent Sales Reporting
  • 02 Fair Expense Documentation
  • 03 Timely Author Payments

Evolution & Expansion

"What began as a financial experiment evolved into a home for emerging voices."

While our roots are in the financial clarity of publishing, our heart has branched out. Today, Clear Ledger is proud to represent tiny, independent, and debut authors across a wide spectrum of genres.

From gripping legal thrillers to experimental debut fiction, we provide the professional backbone that new authors need to turn a manuscript into a legacy.
